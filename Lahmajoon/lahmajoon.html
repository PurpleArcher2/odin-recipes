Lahmahjoon

These savory pizzas are bathed in a tomato and lamb topping, then cut into wedges to serve.
Served hot or cold, these may be an appetizer, or put a little salad in the middle, and fold up
like a taco for a quick meal.

Lahmajoon Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground lamb
  • 1 ½ cups finely chopped onion
  • ½ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can peeled and diced tomatoes
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • ½ cup chopped fresh Italian parsley
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh basil
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint leaves
  • ½ teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper (Optional)
  • 4 pita breads, or fluffy tortillas
